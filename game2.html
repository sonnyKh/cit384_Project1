Blackjack Bonanza

Game Instructions

Blackjack Bonanza is a classic card game where your goal is to beat the dealer without going over 21. Here's how to play:

  1. Place your bet.
  2. Receive two cards and aim for a total of 21 (or as close as possible).
  3. You can "Hit" to receive additional cards or "Stand" to keep your current total.
  4. Beat the dealer's hand to win!
